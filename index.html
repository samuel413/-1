A man shows you into his room.
There is nothing inside, save for a box and a photograph.
The photograph shows another man.
You ask him who is in the photograph, and he responds:

BROTHERS AND SISTERS I HAVE NONE,
BUT THAT MAN'S FATHER IS MY FATHER'S SON.

You ask him what is in the box, and he responds:

YOU PLUS ONE EFF THREE ONE NINE.

You ask him why there's nothing else in the room.

I LEFT EVERYTHING ELSE OUTSIDE,
BY THE THING THAT YOU NEED TO CLICK.
THE SEVEN LETTER WORD THAT CONTAINS MANY MORE LETTERS.

What happens when the contents of the box passes
directly in front of the person in the photograph?
